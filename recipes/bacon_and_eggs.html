Bacon and Egg Recipe

Description

This is my favourite breakfast that I make every other day. It is easy to make and the ingredients are also very cheap making it a perfect breakfast meal.

Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Prepare a pan and heat up and put bacon until almost ready
  2. Depending on individual preference can make bacon very well done or not as burnt and include the eggs.
  3. Eggs can either be scrambled or sunnyside up. All up to preference! Enjoy!
