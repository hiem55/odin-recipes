Pizza Recipe

Description

This is my favorite type of pizza, the acclaimed pepperoni pizza. Filled with rich pepperoni and melted cheese, it is a delightful treat for oneself after a long day at work.

Ingredients

    pepperoni cheese dough tomato sauce add a nice beverage!

Steps

    Prepare dough and make sure to form properly After dough solidifies, apply tomato sauce onto the dough Next make sure to spread cheese on top of the tomato paste Put the pepperoni slices on top of the cheese put the pizza in the oven and wait 30mins Afterwards you can enjoy a nice pizza with a beverage of your choice
