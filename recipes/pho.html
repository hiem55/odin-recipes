Pho Recipe

Description

This is a type of soup I enjoy eating. The broth is very nice and you just can't stop eating once you start!

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Broth Stock
  • Meat
  • Cilantro
  • Beansprout

Steps

  1. Prepare the broth by putting in the stock and boiling it
  2. After broth is made, put in the vegetables and meat
  3. Make sure to start preparing the noodles separately
  4. After the noodles are ready put them in a different bowl and pour the broth. meat and vegetables over and enjoy!
